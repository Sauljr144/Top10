A Different Kind of Underdog Story.

Ford v Ferrari is a 2019 American sports drama film directed by James Mangold. The movie tells the story of the Ford Motor Company's quest to build a car that can beat Ferrari at the 24 Hours of Le Mans race in 1966.

The film features an outstanding cast, with Matt Damon and Christian Bale in the lead roles. Both actors deliver fantastic performances, bringing depth and nuance to their characters. The chemistry between Damon and Bale is excellent, and their scenes together are some of the film's highlights.

The cinematography is impressive, and the racing scenes are well-shot and thrilling to watch. The sound design is also excellent, making the audience feel like they are right in the middle of the action.

The movie does an excellent job of balancing the personal stories of the characters with the high-stakes racing drama. The audience is invested in the characters' journeys, and the film's emotional beats hit hard.

Overall, Ford v Ferrari is an entertaining and engaging film that will satisfy both racing enthusiasts and casual moviegoers alike. It's a well-crafted movie with fantastic performances, impressive visuals, and a compelling story. I highly recommend it.
