THE FOUNDATION

Apple TV+'s Foundation is An Ambitious, Sprawling Sci-Fi Event.

"The Foundation" TV series is a science fiction series based on Isaac Asimov's book series of the same name. The show premiered on Apple TV+ in 2021 and has received generally positive reviews from both fans of the book series and newcomers to the story.

The series follows the story of a group of scientists and mathematicians who are trying to save humanity from a predicted collapse of the Galactic Empire. They establish a Foundation on a remote planet, and as the story unfolds, we see how their plans are challenged by political maneuverings and unexpected events.

The production values of the series are impressive, with stunning visuals and excellent special effects. The acting is also noteworthy, with standout performances from Jared Harris as the lead character Hari Seldon and Lee Pace as the mysterious Emperor of the Galaxy.

The series has been praised for its faithful adaptation of the source material, while also making some changes to better suit the medium of television. Some fans of the book series have criticized the show for deviating too much from the source material, but overall, "The Foundation" TV series has been well-received and is a must-watch for fans of science fiction.
